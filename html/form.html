When creating the visuals for the Time Travel brand we did not want to do a major re-design as Cadillac's logo has recently been changed. Taking inspiration from the main logo and how the V-series logo has been designed, Time Travel hsa a logo that is in line with the rest of the brands. Using the letters TT to create a shape as implents the blend of art and function that is know for the brand. For mordern uses like icons and applications the simplified logo creates a nice modern design. This goes with the unified nature of the brand while offerning a look for different types of user.
